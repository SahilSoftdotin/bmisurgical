What we do

Comprehensive surgical care

From weight-loss surgery to reflux treatment and general surgery, advanced procedures are handled right here in Atlanta.

Weight-Loss Surgery

Gastric sleeve, bypass, mini bypass and duodenal switch — advanced, minimally invasive options.

Reflux & GERD

LINX, TIF, hiatal hernia repair and Stretta to put an end to chronic acid reflux.

Endoscopic Procedures

Gastric balloon, endoscopic sleeve and diagnostic endoscopy — incisionless options.

General Surgery

Hernia repair and gallbladder surgery, performed laparoscopically for a faster recovery.

Medical Weight Loss

Physician-supervised, non-surgical weight-loss programs with nutrition support.

Support Every Step

Pre-op education, post-op care and support groups — guidance through your whole journey.

Meet your surgeon

A leader in advanced weight-loss surgery

Dr. Amir Aryaie is the founder of BMI Surgical Institute and a board-certified general surgeon who has performed thousands of weight-loss and minimally invasive GI procedures. He completed his bariatric & minimally invasive fellowship at University Hospitals / Case Western Reserve.

His approach is compassionate and outcomes-focused — pairing the latest robotic and endoscopic techniques with the personal, attentive care every patient deserves.

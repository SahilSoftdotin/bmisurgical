Meet your surgeon

A leader in advanced weight-loss surgery

Dr. Amir Aryaie is the founder of BMI Surgical Institute and a board-certified general surgeon who has performed thousands of weight-loss and minimally invasive GI procedures. He completed his minimally invasive & bariatric fellowship at University Hospitals / Case Western Reserve in Cleveland.

His approach is compassionate and outcomes-focused — pairing the latest robotic and endoscopic techniques with the personal, attentive care every patient deserves.

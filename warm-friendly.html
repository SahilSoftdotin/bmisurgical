Dr. Amir Aryaie founded BMI Surgical Institute to help Atlanta achieve lasting health. A board-certified surgeon with 16+ years of experience, he has performed thousands of weight-loss and minimally invasive procedures, and completed his bariatric fellowship at University Hospitals / Case Western Reserve.

He takes the time to listen, explain every option clearly, and walk beside you from your first visit through recovery. Around here, you're never just a chart number.
